Seven AI Features

Knowledge Base Integration

Seven AI leverages multiple data storage solutions to provide accurate health information:

Structured Data

PostgreSQL for complex medical data relationships and efficient querying of symptoms, conditions, and treatments.

Lightweight Storage

SQLite for embedded deployments and smaller installations with basic healthcare knowledge requirements.

Flexible Formats

JSON/CSV files for easily updatable reference data like medication lists and symptom databases.

Data Integrity & Safety

Verified Sources Only

All medical information comes from peer-reviewed sources and established medical databases, with strict version control and update protocols.

Clinical Decision Support

Seven AI implements multiple layers of healthcare logic:

Rule-Based Systems

Initial symptom assessment using carefully designed "if-then" rules for common conditions and red flag identification.

Machine Learning

Advanced models trained on clinical datasets provide probabilistic assessments and differential diagnoses.

Clinical Guidelines

Integration with evidence-based medicine protocols ensures recommendations align with current best practices.

System Integrations

Seven AI connects with existing healthcare infrastructure:

EHR Systems

Secure, consent-based access to electronic health records for personalized advice (HL7/FHIR compatible).

Pharmacy Networks

Real-time medication availability checks and prescription renewal support.

Telehealth Platforms

Seamless handoff to human providers when needed, with pre-consultation information gathering.

Emergency Services

Critical situation detection with automatic emergency service notification (where available).

Technical Implementation

Botpress Core

Conversational AI platform handling dialog management, NLU, and user interactions

Custom NLU Models

Healthcare-specific natural language understanding trained on medical dialogues

JavaScript/Python Logic

Code nodes implementing medical decision trees and API integrations

Why Seven AI Matters

Healthcare Access

Bridges gaps for rural populations and those with mobility challenges through 24/7 digital access.

Patient Empowerment

Gives individuals tools and knowledge to actively participate in their healthcare decisions.

Cost Reduction

Reduces unnecessary visits and hospitalizations through accurate preliminary assessments.

Problems We Solve

Information Overload

Curates reliable medical information from trusted sources, eliminating confusion from conflicting online advice.

Care Delays

Helps users recognize when to seek immediate care versus when self-care is appropriate.

Personalization Gap

Provides tailored advice based on individual health profiles rather than generic recommendations.

Medication Adherence

Intelligent reminders and explanations improve compliance with treatment regimens.

Mental Health Support

Provides discreet, stigma-free access to mental health resources and screening tools.
